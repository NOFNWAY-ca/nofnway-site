You've been called lazy. Told to try harder.
Handed "solutions" with seventeen steps and a subscription fee.

This isn't that.

No accounts. No uploads. No cost. No [FN] strings.

I spent 40 years thinking (being told) I just needed to try harder. Turns out I needed a diagnosis and a better keyboard shortcut. I'm not a developer. I'm just someone who got tired of tools that were supposedly simple but weren't, and learned to build better ones. If they help me, they might help you too.

Questions You Might Have

Why is everything free?

Because I've been the person who couldn't afford the tool that would've helped. That's not happening here.

Why no accounts or uploads?

Because you shouldn't have to hand over your data just to get something done. Everything runs in your browser. I never see it.

Who is this for?

Anyone who's ever been excluded from "this is for people who…" — ADHD, anxiety, autism, or just wired differently than the world expected. If the system failed you, you're in the right place.

Is this actually finished?

Some of it. I'm building as fast as my brain and medication allow. Check back — I'm not stopping.

 
I know some stuff is still broken. I'm fixing as fast as my brain vs. medication will allow. Keep checking back — I'll keep building until I get bored or the drugs stop working.
