What This Is

I have severe executive dysfunction. Small annoyances like needing to convert photos to PDF or measure something on screen would completely derail my day, so I built simple tools to remove the friction. They're free. If they help you too, great! If you want to support the work, there's a Ko-fi link below. I could use money to fix my house and buy food and stuff, but no pressure.

 
I know a lot of stuff on this site is still broken, but I'm fixing as fast as my brain vs. medication will allow. Keep checking back in, I'll be making regular improvements until I get bored/the drugs stop working. Thanks for checking it out!
