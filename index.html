Questions You Might Have

Why is everything free?

Real help shouldn't come with a price tag. I know what it's like when the barriers are invisible and the solutions cost money you don't have. If something I've built can help, I'm not going to put it behind a paywall.

If you have something to spare and want to keep the lights on, there's a Ko-fi link below. If you don't, use the tools anyway. That's the whole point.

Why no accounts or uploads?

Because you shouldn't have to hand over your data just to get something done. Everything runs in your browser and stays there unless you say so.

Who is this for?

Anyone who's ever been excluded from "this is for people who..." ADHD, anxiety, autism, wired differently than the world expected. If you need help with the so called "easy" stuff, you're in the right place.

Is this actually finished?

Some of it. I have an idea, I build it, break it, fix it, lather, rinse, repeat until it just works. Check back in, I'm not stopping.

 
I know some stuff is still broken. I'm fixing as fast as my brain vs. medication will allow. Keep checking back. I'll keep building until I get bored or the drugs stop working.
