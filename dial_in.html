Privacy & Data

Nothing you type here is ever sent anywhere. Your info is built entirely in your browser. If you choose Save to device, your field values are stored in your browser's local storage on this device only, not on any server. Session mode (the default) clears everything when you close the tab. Notes are never saved. They exist only while the page is open.

[FN] DIAL IN

The phone rings, your brain suddenly checks out. Next time, bring backup.

Pick a call type. Fill in your details. Cover what matters.

  • Your info on the left: fill in your name and a couple of details specific to the call. The list on the right updates as you type.
  • What to cover on the right: not a script. Just the beats you need to hit, in your own words.
  • During the call: tap the circle on a beat to check it off as you cover it.
  • Tap + on any beat to open a note field. Good for reference numbers, what they said, next steps.
  • Copy notes when you're done. Plain text summary of everything, checked or not, with any notes attached.
  • Save to device (toggle, bottom left of the form) keeps your filled-in details between visits. Off by default.
