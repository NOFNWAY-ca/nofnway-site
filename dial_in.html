Privacy & Data

Nothing you type here is ever sent anywhere. Your script is built entirely in your browser. If you choose Save to device, your field values are stored in your browser's local storage on this device only — not on any server. To remove it, turn off Save to device and reload, or clear site data for nofnway.ca in your browser settings. Session mode (the default) clears everything when you close the tab.
