Four ways to take notes without drowning in them.

  • Quick Capture: just type it. Title, body, subject. Done.
  • Structured: pick a template — Thing, Person, Event, Place, Process, or Q&A. The fields tell you what to write.
  • Cornell: the classic two-column method. Main notes on the left, cues and questions on the right, summary at the bottom.
  • My Notes: everything you've saved, searchable and grouped by subject. Export when you want a backup.
