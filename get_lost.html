Privacy & Data

To generate your route, your approximate location is sent to two open-source services: OpenStreetMap for map data and OSRM for routing. These are the same services behind many free mapping apps. They do not track you, do not know who you are, and do not sell data. Your coordinates leave your device only while the tool is active, and only to generate the route. No location data is stored here.
