Privacy & Data

What you put here stays here. If Save to device is on, your info is stored in your browser's local storage on this device only, not on any server. To remove it, turn off Save to device and reload the page, or open your browser settings, find site data or storage for nofnway.ca, and clear it. Do not use the save option on a shared or public computer.
