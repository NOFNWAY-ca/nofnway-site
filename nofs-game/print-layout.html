Print & Play Generator

This page automatically builds your card sheets from your game's data. To print:

  1. Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P) to open the print dialog.
  2. Set the Destination to "Save as PDF".
  3. Set Layout to "Portrait".
  4. Set Paper Size to "Letter" or "A4".
  5. Under "More settings," ensure "Background graphics" is checked.
  6. Click Save.

You can then print the saved PDF.
