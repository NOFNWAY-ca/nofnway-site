Overview

You have no Fs left to give, but the world hasn't stopped asking. This is a solo game about managing your limited resources (Physical, Social, and Mental) to get through the day (or week) while keeping your Stress from boiling over into Burnout.

Game Modes

  • Single Day: A quick 4-turn game.
  • Full Week: The standard 7-day (28-turn) challenge.
  • Life Mode: Survive for as many days as you can.
