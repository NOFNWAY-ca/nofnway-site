Privacy & Data

Your PDF never leaves your browser. Text is extracted here using PDF.js — the same library Mozilla uses in Firefox. Nothing is sent anywhere. Nothing is saved. Close the tab and it is gone.

[FN] Rewrite

PDFs weren't built for editing. Getting the text back out of one should be a one-step thing. Drop a PDF, get the text, fix what needs fixing, copy it and go.
