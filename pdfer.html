Privacy & Data

Your photos never leave your device. The PDF is built here in your browser using jsPDF. If you use the save feature, your work is kept in your browser's local storage on this device only, not on any server. To remove it, open your browser settings, find site data or storage for nofnway.ca, and clear it.

.pdf

Build a clean PDF from photos, with anything sensitive blacked out.

  • Add Page to add a photo from your camera or files. Add as many as you need.
  • Tap a page to edit it: draw black redaction boxes over anything you don't want to share, or add a page title using the quick-tags (Receipt, Invoice, etc.).
  • Drag pages to reorder them.
  • Tap Make PDF when ready. Your document saves automatically as you work. Come back anytime and pick up where you left off.
  • Nothing is uploaded. Nothing leaves your browser.
