Privacy & Data

Your measurements are saved in your browser's local storage on this device only. Nothing is sent anywhere. To remove them, use the delete options inside the tool, or open your browser settings, find site data or storage for nofnway.ca, and clear it.

A measurement notebook for when you're tired of measuring the same boards twice.

  • Create a project using the project bar at the top. Name it anything.
  • Pick a material type (lumber, pipe, trim, etc.), enter your measurement on the tape, add a description and quantity, then hit MARK.
  • Cut List shows all your measurements grouped by material.
  • Buy List calculates how many standard-length boards (8', 10', 12') you need to buy to cover everything on your cut list. Less guessing at the hardware store.
  • Everything saves automatically in your browser.
