Most people aren't trying to ignore each other. They're just listening for the wrong thing.

Pick a scene. Try a response. Notice what it might feel like from the other side. No grades. No gold stars. Just a little less accidental damage.

Read the room slowly. The point is not to win the moment. It is to notice what the person may be trying to hand you.
