NOFNWAY MAGNET STUDIO

What is this?

A free tool for making custom picture and word tiles at home. Photo magnets, visual schedules, PECS-style communication cards, and literacy word tiles. No account. No uploads. Everything stays on your device.

🖼️ Picture Discs

  1. Choose a shape and size using the dropdowns. Circle for magnets, Square or Rounded for communication cards.
  2. Drag & Drop an image onto any tile, or click the + to browse.
  3. Click a tile to select it, then zoom, rotate, reposition, and add a label.
  4. Use [All] to apply a color or border to every tile at once.
  5. Save your project (💾) and reload it later. Images are saved with the file.

🔤 Word Tiles

  1. Type words separated by spaces. Each word becomes its own tile.
  2. Wrap a phrase in "quotes" to keep it as one tile: "go to bed" → one tile.
  3. Adjust font, style, and colors. Print when ready.
🖨️ Print Tips
  • Always print at 100% / Actual Size. Never "Fit to Page."
  • Enable Background Graphics in the print dialog for fill colors to show.
  • Save as PDF from the print dialog to share or send to a print shop.
  • Use Ctrl+Z / Cmd+Z to undo any change.
